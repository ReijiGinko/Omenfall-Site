Character Builder

Build your hero from the ground up — guided step by step.

1. Choose Your Species

Pick from 15 species. Each grants unique traits, ability score increases, and special features.

2. Select a Class

18 classes with full feature progression from level 1 to 20. Preview everything before you commit.

3. Set Your Background

Your background defines your skills, languages, equipment, and the story of who you were before adventure called.

4. Roll Your Stats

Roll dice, use standard array, or point buy. The builder handles modifiers, proficiencies, and saves automatically.
