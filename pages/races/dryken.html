Dryken

The Dryken are proud beings with the forequarters of great eagles and the hindquarters of lions. They soar above mountain peaks, their keen eyes spotting prey miles below. To serve as a Skyguard is the highest honor; to fly without purpose is to disrespect the wind itself.
